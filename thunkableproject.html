Trivia Game

This program is a trivia game that asks questions from different subjects such as History, Science, and Sports.

The functionality that is being shown in the video is me starting my game and answering some answers correctly and some wrong to show the different functions of the game.

The input is the player pressing the buttons to answer the questions and the output is the boxes letting you know if it is correct or incorrect and then sending you to the next question. Another output is the audio que that you answered a question.
